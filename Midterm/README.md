# Midterm

## Overview
This folder includes my midterm project for my AI in Healthcare class.

## What I Did
For my midterm, I created a glossary of medical terminology used in healthcare. I focused on learning different medical terms and understanding how they are used in real healthcare situations, especially when working with AI.

## What I Learned
This project helped me better understand medical terminology and why it is important in healthcare. I realized that if you don’t understand these terms, it can be hard to understand patient information or how AI systems are being used to support healthcare workers.
